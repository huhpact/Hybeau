iPhone 17 Lineup Leaks: Ultra-Thin Air, Pro Camera Revamp & Reverse Charging

A roundup of the most consistent iPhone 17, 17 Air, and 17 Pro/Pro Max leaks—ultra-thin design, ProMotion across the lineup, new camera layouts, and the long-rumored reverse wireless charging.

Paul C. Content Manager
August 26, 2025 9 min read

Apple’s “Awe Dropping” keynote is set for September 9, 2025, and leaks paint a bold picture of the iPhone 17 family. Expect an all-new ultra-thin iPhone 17 Air, Pro models with redesigned cameras and aluminum builds, and the long-awaited reverse wireless charging.

The Lineup

The iPhone 17 series will reportedly consist of four models: iPhone 17, iPhone 17 Air, iPhone 17 Pro, and iPhone 17 Pro Max. The “Plus” model is being retired, replaced by the slim and lightweight Air variant.

iPhone 17 Air

The iPhone 17 Air is rumored to be Apple’s thinnest phone ever—around 5.5 mm—with a 6.6-inch 120 Hz ProMotion OLED display. It may feature a centered horizontal camera bar with a single 48 MP sensor, a titanium-aluminum frame, Wi-Fi 7, and eSIM-only support. Reports suggest Apple may revive the iPhone 4-style bumper case to protect the slim design.

iPhone 17 Air Key Specs

  • 5.5 mm thin chassis, ~145 g weight
  • 6.6″ OLED, 120 Hz ProMotion
  • A19 chip, 12 GB RAM
  • Wi-Fi 7, eSIM only
  • 48 MP single rear camera

iPhone 17 Pro & Pro Max

Pro models may switch to aluminum frames, with half-glass backs and a rectangular camera bar housing a triangular triple-lens setup. Expect A19 Pro chips, 12 GB RAM, improved thermal cooling, and enhanced camera hardware—including a 24 MP front camera and a 48 MP telephoto lens with up to 8× zoom. The Pro Max may also enable 8K video recording.

Reverse Wireless Charging

After years of speculation, reverse wireless charging could finally arrive on the iPhone 17 Pro models. This feature would allow users to wirelessly charge AirPods, Apple Watch, and other Qi-compatible devices directly from their iPhone.

Design & Colors

Pro models may arrive in bold new colors such as copper-orange and deep blue, while the iPhone 17 Air could debut in light blue. All models are expected to feature slimmer bezels and uniform ProMotion displays.

Pricing & Release

Apple is expected to open pre-orders on September 12, with deliveries beginning around September 19. Pricing is rumored to start at the iPhone 17 base model’s $799 equivalent, with the Air positioned just below the Pro tier.

Conclusion

Apple’s iPhone 17 lineup signals one of its boldest updates yet—pushing thinness with the Air and adding long-awaited features to the Pro models. If these leaks hold true, the September 9 event will mark a significant step forward in Apple’s design and innovation story.
